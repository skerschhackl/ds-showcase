# Molecule: Card

Use `Card` for soft grouped content such as metric summaries, workflow sections, generated preview modules, and repeated item surfaces.

## Approved Props

- Native `div` props
- Optional `className`

## AI Rules

- Keep Card content focused on one concept.
- Do not nest cards inside cards.
- Prefer Card for summaries, metrics, or repeated items, not full page sections.
- Use `--ds-space-6` as the default internal padding when composing custom card content.
- Use larger specimen-like cards for component examples instead of many tiny dense cards.
